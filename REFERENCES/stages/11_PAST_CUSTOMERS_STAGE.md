## 3. Logic Engines: The Lifetime Partner

### The "Asset Guardian" (C-10)
- **Mechanism**: The customer portal evolves. Instead of a "Project Profile," it becomes a "Home Asset Manager."
- **Data**: Stores roof age, shingle types, warranty serial numbers, and every photo ever taken during the build.
- **Value**: Homeowner can one-click export a "Transferable Warranty Package" if they sell their house (Massive Selling Point).

### The "Referral Tier" Protocol (Abundance Engine)
- **Tiers**:
  - **Tier 1 (Scout)**: 1 Referral Signed -> $500 Payout.
  - **Tier 2 (Partner)**: 3 Referrals Signed -> $2,000 Bonus + Free Gutter Cleaning for life.
  - **Tier 3 (Rhive Legend)**: 10 Referrals -> Lifetime Inspection Priority + Direct Founder Line.

### The "Secondary Service" Upsell (E-50)
- **Trigger**: System identifies customers whose roof is >3 years old.
- **Action**: AI sends a "Service Tune-up" offer: "Hey [Name], your Rhive roof is 3 years old today! We're in your area next Tuesday doing a Gutter Audit. Want us to pop by for a free sealant check?"

## 4. Operational Commands: The Evergreen Heartbeat
- **Annual Wellness Check**: Pro-active reaching out *without* a sales pitch to maintain the "Dove" (Safety/Harmony) frame.
- **Holiday Recognition**: Automated branded mailers/texts to top-tier ambassadors.

## 5. UI Directive: The Abundance Anchor
- **Visuals**: A "Lifetime Partner" badge displayed on the Home Vault.
- **CTA**: "Share the Abundance: Gift a Neighbor $500 Off."
