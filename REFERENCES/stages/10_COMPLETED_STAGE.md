## 3. Logic Engines: The Advocacy Machine

### The "Warranty Vault" (Paid Gate Logic)
- **Automatic Fulfillment**: Once the system detects `Balance == 0`:
  - 1. Merges `Product_Manifest` + `Final_Photos` + `Manufacturer_Docs` into a "Rhive Certified" PDF.
  - 2. Unlocks the **C-04 (Warranty & Care)** page for the homeowner.
  - 3. Sends SMS: "Your home is now fully protected. Your Rhive Warranty Vault is now open! [Link]"

### The "Referral Engine" ($500 Program)
- **The "Social Bridge"**: In the portal, AI generates a "Project Story" (Before/After collage + "Roofing for Good" impact stats).
- **Gamification**: If customer clicks "Share to Facebook/Nextdoor," their account is tagged as a `BRAND_AMBASSADOR`.
- **Payout Bot**: If a new Lead enters via their unique ID and signs, the system triggers a **"Referral Bonus Pending"** notification to the owner.

### The "Marketing Syndicator" (E-40)
- **Logic**: System identifies high-sentiment projects (based on photo quality and timeline speed).
- **Action**: Auto-generates a "Local Case Study" for the website: "How we fixed [Customer Name]'s roof in [Neighborhood] in 48 hours."
